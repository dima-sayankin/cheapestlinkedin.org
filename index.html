I got tired of overpaying for Premium — so I found a hack.

I got fed up when I saw that LinkedIn charges 3–4× more in some countries for the exact same subscription. It’s not about features — it’s pure geography pricing. So I built this page to find out where Premium is cheapest and share it with everyone.

Whether you’re hunting for a job, building your network, or just curious — you deserve Premium at a fair price, so you can invest in yourself, not in LinkedIn’s pricing games.

How to get the cheapest price

  1. 1) Open your LinkedIn Profile → Settings & Privacy.
  2. 2) Go to Name, location & industry, scroll down to Location, set the country you want (e.g., the cheapest from the list), and save.
  3. 3) Visit the Premium selection page: linkedin.com/premium/products and pick your plan.
  4. 4) Once you’ve subscribed, go back to your Settings → Location and switch it back to your actual country — the one where you’re applying for jobs or building your network.
